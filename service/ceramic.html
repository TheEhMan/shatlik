Ceramic Coating

Unmatched gloss and hydrophobic protection.

Liquid Glass Protection

Ceramic coating applies a semi-permanent layer of protection to your vehicle's paint. This nano-technology bonds with the clear coat to create a hydrophobic surface that repels water, dirt, and road grime.

Say goodbye to waxing. Ceramic coating filters UV rays to prevent fading, resists chemical stains, and provides a deep, wet-look gloss that makes your car stand out.

Key Benefits

  • Intense hydrophobic water beading
  • Chemical and UV resistance
  • Easier maintenance washing
  • Enhanced depth and gloss
