Scratch Removing

Restore your vehicle's flawless finish.

Expert Paint Correction

Scratches, swirls, and holograms can dull your vehicle's paint and ruin its appearance. Our scratch removal service uses advanced paint correction techniques to permanently eliminate imperfections, not just hide them.

We assess the depth of the damage and use the appropriate combination of compounds and polishes to restore a mirror-like deep reflection.

Key Benefits

  • Permanently removes swirls and scratches
  • Restores color depth and gloss
  • Increases vehicle resale value
  • Prepares paint for ceramic coating
