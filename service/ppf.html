Paint Protection Film (PPF)

Invisible armor for your vehicle.

Ultimate Impact Protection

Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a clear, thermoplastic urethane film applied to painted surfaces of a new or used car in order to protect the paint from stone chips, bug splatters, and minor abrasions.

Our self-healing film ensures that minor scratches disappear with heat, keeping your car looking brand new for years to come.

Key Benefits

  • Self-healing properties
  • Crystal clear invisible finish
  • Protects against rock chips
  • 10-year warranty available
